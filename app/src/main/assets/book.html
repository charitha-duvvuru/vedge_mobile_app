BOOK EXPLORER CLUB

 

OBJECTIVES:

  • To create a milieu for students who wish to explore the world of books.
  • To help students imbibe the habit of reading books
  • To organize lectures and industrial visits to motivate the young designers and players.
  • Encourage students to write brief book summary reports which will aid easy recall, intelligent discussion and application of these lessens to their life and work.

 

EXPECTED OUTCOMES:

  • Students will be able to critique and discuss the central idea / core concepts in the book
  • Articulate questions that can lead to a thoughtful, deep discussion
  • Collaborate with Book Club members to generate solutions to the problems exposed in the book and related problems witnessed in the society
