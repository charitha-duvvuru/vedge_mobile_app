DANCE CLUB

OBJECTIVES:

  • To develop an understanding of the artistic and technical elements of physical expression that lead to performance artistry, and will attain and/or maintain a technical proficiency, based on sound kinesiological principles, at the high intermediate level of at least two of the following techniques: 21st Century Dance principles, theory and practice like contemporary Indian Bollywood, Contemporary Indian Folk Dance, Ballet, African, Flamenco, Jazz, Odissi, Bharatyanatyam.
  • To develop a career in dancing for those who have the passion.
  • To provide opportunities for healthful relaxation and recreation, grace and rhythm, coordination of body movements, maintenance of fitness and body posture, and proper body mechanics.

 

EXPECTED OUTCOMES:

  • Gain better understanding of the use of their bodies
  • Students will learn the values and methods of artistic collaboration.
  • student will be prepared to enter the professional world of performing/theatrical arts.
