INNOVATORS CLUB

 

 

OBJECTIVES: 

  • To open up the INNOVATION knowledge in students.
  • This club will improve the student knowledge about various leading and emerging areas in the domain of their interest.
  • This club will provide opportunity for students to present their ideas.

 

EXPECTED OUTCOMES: 

  • Students will develop interest on research and innovation.
  • The students will find good research problem in their research area.
  • Student will improve research paper writing technique and will publish good research papers.
