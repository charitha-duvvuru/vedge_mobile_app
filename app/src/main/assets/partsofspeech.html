PARTS OF SPEECH

 

There are EIGHT Parts of Speech, 8 in number.

The WORDS are divided into different kinds, they are -

1. NOUN

2. PRONOUN

3. VERB

4. ADJECTIVE

5. ADVERB

6. PREPOSITION

7. CONJUNCTION

8. INTERJECTION


NOUN:- It is a word used as the name of a Person, Place or Thing.

               EXPL:- 1.Gandhi was a great leader.
              2.Kolkata is a largest city .
              3.The Rose smells sweet .
              4.The Sun shines bright  .


 PROUNOUN:- It is a WORD used instead of a NOUN.
 EXPL:- Rajesh is absent, because he is ill.


 VERB:- It is a WORD used to express an ACTION or STATE [sthithi].
 EXPL:-    1.Rani WROTE a letter to her Cousin.
              2.The team PLAYED the match Yesterday.
              3.The Girl DANCED well.


 ADJECTIVE:-   It’s a WORD used to add SOMETHING or SOME MEANING to  the NOUN [person, place,              trees & animals and things.]
 EXPL:-   1.RAJESH/HE is a BRAVE BOY
             2.BOMBAY is a BIGGEST CITY.
             3.TIGER is a Ferocious  Animal


ADVERB:-   It is a WORD used to add SOMETHING or SOME MEANING to a
                   VERB, an ADJECTIVE or to another ADVERB.
 EXPL:- 1. He WORKED the Sum QUICKLY.
            2.Rose is VERY BEAUTIFUL
            3.She pronounced the Word QUITE CORRECTLY.


 PREPOSITION:- It is a WORD used with a NOUN or a PRONOUN to express or show the POSITION                        STATE of a PERSON or THING in relation to something else.
 EXPL:- 1.There is a Horse in the ground.
             2.The Girl is fond of Music.
             3.The old Man sat under the tree.     


CONJUNCTION:- It is a WORD used to join WORDS or SENTENSES
  EXPL:- 1. Raju and Ravi are friends.
              2. Raju Or Ravi can solve the sum./ is absent in the class.  
              3. I ran fast, But I missed the Train.


INTERJECTION:- It is a WORD which EXPRESSES some sudden FEELINGS.
 EXPL:- 1. Hurray, We have won the watch.
             2. Oh, Ha, Wah, Wow, Warrey Wah etc.
