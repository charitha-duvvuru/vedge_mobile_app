PREPOSITIONS

 

Preposition is a Word used with a Noun or a Pronoun to Express or Show the Position or State of a Person or Thing in Relation to Something Else.         

        

They are 3 Kinds:

                                     1. Simple Prepositions

                                     2. Compound Prepositions

                                     3. Phrase Prepositions


Simple Prepositions :- in, on, at, by, of, to, for, from, with, out etc.


Compound Prepositions :- about, above, across, along, among, before, around, behind, below, beside, between, inside, outside, with in/out .


Phrase Prepositions :- according to, along with, away from, instead of  in place of, because of, by means of, by reason of, for the sake of, in lieu of, in case of, in addition to, in favour of, in order to, in regard to etc .


Simple Prepositions:-  1. The Children are Playing in the ground

                                      2. The Book is on the Table.

                                      3. I Should complete the Work by tomorrow.


Compound Prepositions :- 1. They waited about an Hour for me.

                                              2. The Books are inside the Bag.

                                              3. The Students must enroll between 9 am and 5pm, today.


Phrase Prepositions :- 1.You are absent for 5 days according to the  attendance  register.

                                       2. He lost his Job during recession along with 100’s of others.

                                      3.We just had snacks instead of a full Meal.
