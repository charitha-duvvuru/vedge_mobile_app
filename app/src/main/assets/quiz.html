QUIZ CLUB

 

OBJECTIVES: 

  • Increase the level of general awareness amongst the student community.
  • Quint essential awareness of Science, Literature, Technology, and Current Affairs outside the realm of the formal syllabus.
  • Promote their Intelligence Quotient (IQ) and Quizzing ability.

 

EXPECTED OUTCOMES: 

  • Members will enhance their observation and creative thinking skills.
  • They are motivated and self-reliant.
  • They get habituated with brainstorming for ideas on daily basis.
  • They can face any type of interviews easily if they know out of the curriculum.
